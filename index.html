Sylhet Polytechnic Institute

Sylhet Government Polytechnic Institute, Sylhet,Bangladesh is an engineering educational
institution regulated by the Bangladesh Technical Education Board. This Government Engineering
Institute was established in the year 1955 in Punyabhoomi Sylhet city of 360 Aulia with a huge area.

About Our Campus

It is established 1955; 68 years ago 20 acres (8.1 ha)all over the place.In 1955, Ford Foundation established Sylhet Polytechnic Institute along with four other similar institutes at Dhaka, Rangpur, Bogra, Pabna and Barisal. At the beginning, SPI offered 3 years long courses, based on the syllabus of Oklahoma State University. The certificate issued by the then Technical Education Board was Associated in Engineering having provision to undergo Bachelor of Science courses in United States. Its formation and academic course history goes back to the birth of Ahsanullah Engineering College, which is now known as BUET. The campus of SPI was designed by Muzharul Islam and Stanley Tigerman.
